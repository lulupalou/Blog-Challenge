# Facebook **Love story**
<img alt="facebook status" src="/images/Match.png" width="1000" />

In late 2019, as a midwest cuffing season set in, a Facebook Dating match changed everything. It started with a single emoji and a line that was either genius or a total accident. "The awkward moment when you accidentally send a thumbs up. Hi!"

The verdict? "Never heard that before, but it works. Boba?"

The first date was a true marathon. It began with Herreld pulling up, music blasting, and greeting Palou with the now iconic compliment, "Wow, you look so fall!" What was supposed to be a quick bowling and boba date turned into an eight-hour adventure.

They moved from the lanes at Revel & Roll to boba at Chocolatea, and then in a classic move to keep the night alive an impromptu walk through the aisles of Meijer. Still not ready to say goodbye, they shared plum wine at Hunan Gardens, finally ending the night at a Taco Bell parking lot where they talked until the early morning hours. Six years and countless milestones later, the conversation hasn't stopped.
